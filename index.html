About CRC.

Since our inception, Climatic Resources Consulting has been focused on providing communities and organisations with detailed climate histories and localized climate model projections — and facilitating the planning process by providing quantitative assessments of the likelihood of critical threshold exceedance for infrastructure and operations.

Based in the West Kootenay region of British Columbia, Climatic Resources Consulting (CRC) is a data-driven, climate resilience software and consulting company. We provide leading edge climate projections and quantitative risk analysis that facilitate the prioritisation of climate risks within asset management structures.

The approach and related software — Localised Climate Analysis and Projections (LCap) — uses statistical downscaling and probabilistic analysis to generate site-specific climate projections. LCap enables planners and engineers to use a probability-based approach to undertake targeted climate change planning for specific community operations and infrastructure.

Services.

CRC offers targeted, data-driven climate consulting services designed for communities, engineers, and asset managers working in a changing climate.

Climate Projections

Detailed climate histories and localised, statistically-downscaled climate model projections. We provide site-specific, multi-decadal climate outlooks grounded in observational data and the latest global model ensembles.

Quantitative Risk Analysis

Probability-based analysis of the likelihood of critical climate threshold exceedance for specific operations and infrastructure. We translate climate science into actionable risk metrics for decision-makers.

Asset Management Support

Integration of climate risk assessments into asset management frameworks, enabling long-term resilience planning and informed capital investment decisions for infrastructure operating under changing climate conditions.

LCap Software

Our proprietary Localised Climate Analysis and Projections (LCap) software delivers a powerful, probabilistic tool for site-specific climate change planning — purpose-built for planners and engineers.

About.

Mel Reasoner PhD

Founder & Principal Scientist

Mel Reasoner PhD is the founder and principal scientist of Climatic Resources Consulting, based in Nelson, British Columbia — at the heart of the West Kootenay region. His work bridges rigorous climate science with the practical demands of infrastructure planning, helping communities and organisations understand and plan for a changing climate.

With deep roots in the mountains and valleys of the BC interior, Mel brings both scientific expertise and a grounded, place-based perspective to climate resilience work. His approach reflects the realities faced by rural communities, resource industries, and engineers who must plan for decades ahead in a landscape shaped by weather, water, and seasonal variability.

CRC's methods and LCap software grew directly from Mel's research into quantitative climate analysis — translating rigorous scientific methodology into practical tools for planners, engineers, and asset managers across Canada and beyond.
